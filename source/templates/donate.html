How Can You Contribute?

Make A Contribution Today

With this year's project having an outreach of 220 Timorese children and youths (way more than the 40 children and youths we reached out to in 2023!), we will greatly appreciate any amount given cheerfully to the Lord.

To support us financially:

  1. Please scan the QR code and indicate diliyouth as your reference.
  2. Afterwards, please screenshot the payment screen and send it to May Yen Tan (98303185) via Whatsapp.

Your contributions will be directed towards:

  • Improving the infrastructure of Cocoon to protect against flooding, as well as provide a safe and conducive place for children and youths to learn.
  • Blessing the children with gifts such as snacks, clothes, toys and stationery.

100% of the contributions from these cats will also be benefitting the Timorese children and youths.
